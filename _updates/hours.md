---
title: New Library Hours in Fall 2026
date: 2026-05-05
---

In fall 2026, the Library will implement new hours, remaining open from 7am to midnight most weekdays.
This change in hours will better align with library usage and traffic patterns.

### Fall 2026 Hours
{:.h4}

- Monday - Thursday: 7am - midnight
- Friday: 7am - 8pm
- Saturday: noon - 6pm
- Sunday: noon - midnight
