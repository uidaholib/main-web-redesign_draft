title: Participate in a Study of Student Perceptions of Research and Writing
date: 2026-01-12
---

The Research Help Desk and Writing Center are conducting a voluntary, IRB-approved
survey of student perceptions of research and writing. The survey will take about 15
minutes to complete. No risks are associated with this study.

[>> click here to take the survey <<](https://uidaho.co1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_daPxOy95lrYOpaS)

Who can participate?
- Currently enrolled undergraduate students at the University of Idaho
- No experience using the Research Help Desk or Writing Center is needed
- Must be at least 18 years old

Why participate?
- Enter to win one of four **$50 Vandal Store gift cards**
- Help us understand how to improve research and writing services for you

Questions? Contact the researchers:
- [Celia Hagey](https://www.lib.uidaho.edu/about/people/celiahagey.html), Instruction & User Services Librarian: <celiahagey@uidaho.edu>
- [Emma Catherine Perry](https://verso.uidaho.edu/esploro/profile/emma_perry/overview), Writing Center Director: <emmaperry@uidaho.edu>
