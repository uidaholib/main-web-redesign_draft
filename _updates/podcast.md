[Open Invitation](https://cdil.lib.uidaho.edu/open-invitation/) is a new podcast that highlights open access efforts from across the University of Idaho. 
Interviews feature U of I faculty, staff, and students discussing their cutting-edge research, as well as the benefits and challenges of navigating the open access landscape.

Three episodes are available now, visit the [Open Invitation website](https://cdil.lib.uidaho.edu/open-invitation/) to find all the ways to listen and subscribe!
