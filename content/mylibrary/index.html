What Does Your Library Look Like (video)

The My Library campaign features timely, real-life library stories highlighting University of Idaho students and little-known library services by asking, "What does your library look like?" We hope sharing real student experiences will help reveal solutions to your educational needs and expand your perception of the possibilities at the library!

The U of I Library is known mainly for books and studying but that perception is not the whole story. The library is a foundational powerhouse of information, ideas, tools, services, spaces, and more, available to EVERY U of I student. Browse our featured stories or learn more below.

My Library

Library perks include beautifully comfortable spaces for groups and resources such as:

  • 3D printing
  • green screen and lighting
  • virtual reality, an audio/visual lab
  • electronic textbooks and course materials that add up to great savings
  • helpful librarians
  • board games
  • immediate access to millions of physical and electronic books and journals
  • digital georeferenced aerial imagery
  • historical collections
  • topic experts
  • tens of thousands of DVDs and streaming videos
  • awesome workshops
