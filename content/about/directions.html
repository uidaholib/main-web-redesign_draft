Parking Options

Parking on campus is managed by Parking and Transportation Services with most spaces requiring payment or a permit.

Pay to Park

There is a mix of self-serve pay station parking and metered parking in close proximity to the Library. Both of these options allow for payment with debit/credit cards and coins. Hourly rates vary based on the location and time of day and range from $0.75 to $1.50 per hour. Consult the campus parking maps for up to date details and locations.

Visitor Permit

If you are a temporary visitor to campus, Visitor permits are available for $3/day to allowing you to park in lots or any marked Visitor space on campus. Visit the campus Visitors page for details.

Overnight Parking

An annual or temporary overnight permit may be purchased for use by those who reside off campus and need to park on campus between the hours of 2:00 am and 7:45 am. Pricing and purchasing instructions can be found on Parking's Other Permits page.

Safe Walk

Leaving late? Take advantage of U of I's Free 24-7 Campus Security Walking Service.

Call (208) 885-7054 or (208) 874-7550 for assistance.
