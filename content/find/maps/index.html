Map Resources

Whether you are looking for the most up-to-date geospatial information or a unique visual aid for a history presentation, the University of Idaho Library Cartographic Collections have a number of resources for you to use! The Map Room Hours are Monday through Friday 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. (7:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. during the summer), with weekend hours available by appointment. Digital Maps via Digital Initiatives are available around the clock.
