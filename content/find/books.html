Finding Books in the U of I Library

Print books can be found in the library arranged by their Library of Congress call numbers. Consult our floor maps and LOC reference to find where they are located.

Ebooks can be accessed through our catalog from anywhere in the world using your U of I login. Check our Finding eBooks page for more details about access.

Not finding what you're looking for? Suggest a book for us to add to our collections using our Purchase Request Form.
