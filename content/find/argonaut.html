The Argonaut is the award winning student newspaper produced by the University of Idaho Student Media in the Department of Student Involvement. Continuously in print since 1898, it was historically published twice a week during the academic year and distributed across campus and throughout Moscow, Idaho and Pullman, Washington. The Argonaut continues to publish a print edition, as well as the Argonaut online edition.

Library holdings include issues of Argonaut in print, microfilm, and digital. Bound paper copies may be used in the Special Collections reading room. The full microfilm back file is located on the library's third floor. Access to digital scans of issues from 1898 to 2000 are found in the Argonaut Digital Collection. Content since Fall 2000+ may be found at the current Argonaut online edition.
