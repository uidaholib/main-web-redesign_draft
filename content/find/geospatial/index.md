## Software 

The Library provides access and support for geospatial software, including managing our institutional license for Esri GIS. 
Visit our [GIS software page]({{ '/find/geospatial/software.html' | relative_url }}) to find more information and downloads:

## Data

- [U of I ArcGIS Online Teaching & Scholarly Research Portal](https://uidaho.maps.arcgis.com) - Data and apps shared across or outside our organization. Additionally, explore the ArcGIS Living Atlas of the World.
- [INSIDE Idaho](https://insideidaho.org) - Open data and apps from publishers across Idaho.
- [U of I Geocatalog](https://geocatalog-uidaho.hub.arcgis.com/) - Data and apps from U of I publishers. Sign in to access licensed datasets.
- [VERSO Data Repository](https://verso.uidaho.edu/) - U of I research and creative outputs.
- Share your Geospatial Data - Contact [Bruce Godfrey](mailto:bgodfrey@uidaho.edu) about sharing your geospatial datasets using Web GIS.

## Teaching and Learning

- [Library Workshops]({{ '/events/' | relative_url }}) - geospatial related sessions are regularly offered through our Data Hub workshop series.
- [GeoInquiries](https://www.esri.com/en-us/industries/education/schools/geoinquiries-collections) - Short, standards-based inquiry activities for teaching map-based content.
- [Esri Self-Paced E-Learning Courses](https://www.esri.com/training/) - Students, faculty, and staff are afforded access to no-cost self-paced e-learning resources (Sign in using your U of I Account).
- [GIS Education Guide](https://www.gisdegree.org/) - Search for GIS degrees & certificate programs in the USA.
- [Working with GIS data](https://onlinegrad.syracuse.edu/blog/gis-data/)

## Communities

- [State of Idaho Geotech Listserv](https://survey123.arcgis.com/share/4cd053e4ee6547eab85ca795e3219491)
- [Esri Higher Education Community](https://www.esri.com/en-us/industries/higher-education/community)
- [Esri User Conference Complimentary Registrations](https://www.esri.com/en-us/about/events/uc/overview) - A limited number of complimentary registrations are available on a first come, first served basis to those affiliated with the U of I. Email [Bruce Godfrey](mailto:bgodfrey@uidaho.edu) for more information.
- [GIS Day @ U of I]({{ '/gisday/' | relative_url }}) - annual event on campus each fall.
