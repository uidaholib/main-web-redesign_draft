--- title: ArcGIS Pro and OneDrive section: Find permalink: /find/geospatial/arcgisproandonedrive.html layout: page ---

Even though it's technically possible, accessing files directly from OneDrive with ArcGIS Pro is not supported by Esri because it may result in errors. See the 'Solution and Workaround' section of Esri’s official statement on using ArcGIS Pro with OneDrive to avoid potential problems.
