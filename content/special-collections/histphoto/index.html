The Historical Photograph Database consists of a table of over 90,000 images held by Special Collections.

Tips on Using HistPhoto

  • The table displays a maximum of 500 images per query; the search boxes and group limiters on the left will help you limit your results.
  • The search box only searches stings of text (i.e. consecutive words), so using one precise term is your best bet for finding the image you seek.

For further help, contact Special Collections via email at libspec@uidaho.edu or by phone at (208) 885-0845
