Librarians can help you efficiently and effectively search and use library resources and other information sources. Each college at U of I has a designated subject librarian who is ready to help with all your research and library questions.

To request a consultation with a librarian, please find your subject liaison below and click the "Request Meeting" button to get in touch. If you are unsure or need immediate assistance, contact our Research Help Desk and we will make sure your questions get answered.
