Students can get overwhelmed in the world of information, and can benefit from library faculty teaching skills for efficiently finding and evaluating information. The U of I Library offers the a variety of instructional services for students and faculty.

English 102 Instruction

Students in English 102 will get a basic introduction to information research in connection with their research paper assignment. See our English 102 Research Guide for details.

Information Literacy Instruction

Students often need additional instruction in more sophisticated information sources for particular assignments or disciplines in other courses. Librarians can lead classroom sessions to help!

Contact your library liaison or fill out our Instruction Request form.
