Instruction Services

Students can get overwhelmed in the world of information, and can benefit from library faculty teaching skills for efficiently finding and evaluating information. The UI Library offers the following instructional services for students and faculty:

English 102 Instruction

Students in English 102 will get a basic introduction to information research in connection with their research paper assignment.See our English 102 Research Guide.

English 102 instructors can get additional information via our instructors page.

Information Literacy in other courses

Students often need additional instruction in more sophisticated information sources for particular assignments or disciplines.

Contact your library liaison or fill out this form to request a session.

Research Assistance Program

Faculty and students are encouraged to make an appointment with a librarian when individual research assistance is needed.
